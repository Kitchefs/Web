Choc-Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 125g Butter
  • ½ Cup Brown Sugar
  • ⅓ Cup Caster Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 ½ Cups Self Raising Flour
  • ¾ Cup Milk Chocolate Choc-Chips

Makes: 30

Preparation: 15

Cook Time: 15

Total Time: 35

Method:

  1. Line baking tray, preheat Oven to 160°C.
  2. Cream together Butter and Sugar until pale.
  3. Wisk in Egg and Vanilla.
  4. Stir in Flour and Choc-Chips.
  5. Bake for 10-15 minutes, Leave to cool.
