    1. Create 3 initial sketch ideas  showing at least 2 views for each different idea.
    2. Annotate each in terms of aerodynamic design, indicating your preferred design.
    3. Scan or take a clear photo of your initial sketches and annotations
    4. Create a refined sketch showing necessary views detailing all dimensions
    5. Scan or photograph your refined sketch
    6. Collate initial sketches and annotations and refined sketches onto one Word document for submission.
