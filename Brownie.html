Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 125g Cup Butter
  • 1 Cup Caster Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • ⅓ Cup Coca Powder
  • ½ Cup Plain Flour
  • ¼ Tsp Salt
  • ¼ Tsp Baking Powder

Makes: Amount

Preparation: 20 Minutes

Cook Time: 30 Minuites

Total Time: 55 Minutes

Method:

  1. Line baking tray, preheat oven to 180°C,
  2. In a medium sized bowl, cream Butter and Sugar on high speed until pale.
  3. Mix in Eggs and Vanilla Extract, until just combined.
  4. On low speed beat in Coca Powder, Flour, Salt and Baking Powder until smooth.
  5. Cook for 25-30 minutes. Leave to cool.
