Chocolate Mud Cake

Ingredients:

  • 3 Cups Hot Water
  • 500g Unsalted Butter
  • 400g Milk Chocolate
  • ½ Cup Cocoa Powder
  • 4 Cups Caster Sugar
  • 4 Eggs
  • 4 Tsp Vanilla Essence
  • 3 Cups Self Rasing Flour
  • 3 Cups Icing Sugar
  • ⅓ Cup Butter, softened
  • 2 Tsp Vanilla Essence, Extra
  • 2 Tbs Milk

Yield: 15

Preparation: 30 Minutes

Cook Time: 1 Hour

Total Time: 1 Hour, 40 Minutes

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 160 °C, Line 3 cake tins with baking paper.
  2. In a saucepan over low heat melt together Butter, Cocoa, Chocolate, Sugar, Vanilla and Water.
  3. Leave to cool and add in Flour and Eggs, mix well.
  4. Split evenly into the 3 prepred tins.
  5. Bake for 1 hour and ensure knife comes out clean before removing from oven. Leave to cool.
  6. To make the Buttercream Icing Combine Icing Sugar, Buter, Vanilla and Milk until smooth.
  7. Stack the cakes, spreading the Buttercream Icing between each layer and coating the outside.
  8. You can sprinkle on grated Chocolate, Sprinkles, Strawberries or Lollies for decoration.
